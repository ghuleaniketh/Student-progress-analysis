## 🚀 Student Performance Analyser

Most students don’t struggle because they lack ability — they struggle because they lack clarity.

They don’t know what to study, what to ignore, or where they’re actually weak.  
So they either waste time on the wrong topics or freeze under pressure.

**Student Performance Analyser fixes that.**

This is not just a marks calculator.  
It’s an **AI-powered academic decision system** that turns raw scores into clear, actionable study strategies.

Instead of telling you:
> “You scored 72%”

It tells you:
> “You’re weak in these topics, your exam is close, focus here first — ignore this for now.”

---

### 🧠 What Makes It Different

- **Priority-Based Intelligence**  
  Combines marks + exam dates to decide what actually matters *right now*

- **Weakness Detection**  
  Automatically identifies weak, average, and strong subjects

- **Actionable Study Strategy**  
  Not just analysis — gives you a clear plan of what to do next

- **AI-Powered Feedback**  
  Generates personalised study guidance based on your performance

- **Voice Interaction**  
  Listen to your study plan or ask questions using speech

- **Syllabus-Aware Recommendations**  
  Uses uploaded PDFs to give context-aware suggestions (not generic advice)

---

### 🎯 The Goal

To eliminate confusion and give students **clarity under pressure**.

Because the real problem isn’t studying harder —  
it’s knowing **what actually deserves your effort**.
